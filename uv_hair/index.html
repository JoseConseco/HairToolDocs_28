Hair UV¶

You can uv map curve ribbons by going into UV editor and search for ‘Hair UV’ operator under spacebar.

Left click and draw rectangular shape, in uv Editor, to define the uv bounds for hair strands. Or just drag corners of existing uv box to adjust its shape.
